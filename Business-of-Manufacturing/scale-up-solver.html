Scenario Brief

ClearPath Medical has just received 510(k) clearance for the HemoSeal bioprosthetic aortic valve. Demand has surged to 1,000,000 units/year. Your current factory is built for 200,000 units/year. You have a $2,000,000 capital budget and 12 months to close the gap before competitors enter the market. Every month of delay costs market share — and every regulatory misstep costs far more.
